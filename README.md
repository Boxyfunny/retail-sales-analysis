# Retail Sales Performance Analysis (90 Days)

## 📊 Overview
This project analyzes 90 days of retail sales data to evaluate revenue, profitability, and business performance across products, categories, and regions.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To identify key drivers of revenue and profit, and provide actionable insights to improve business performance.

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Date
- Product
- Category
- Units Sold
- Price per Unit
- Cost per Unit
- Region

---

## 🧮 Key Metrics
- Revenue
- Total Cost
- Profit
- Profit Margin

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- Profit by Category
- Profit by Product
- Revenue by Region
- Sales Trend over 90 Days

---

## 📊 Dashboard
A dashboard was created in Excel to visualize:
- Key KPIs (Revenue, Profit, Margin)
- Category performance
- Sales trends
- Product performance

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- High-volume products generate strong revenue but lower margins
- High-margin products contribute more to profitability
- Certain regions outperform others consistently
- Sales trends show patterns that can guide future strategy

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on high-margin products
- Optimize cost-heavy categories
- Expand in high-performing regions
- Monitor trends for growth opportunities

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Google Sheets (Pivot Tables, Charts, Dashboard)
